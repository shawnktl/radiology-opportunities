# Educational Courses

AI/ML courses, statistics workshops, leadership programs, and other formal educational opportunities relevant to radiology trainees.

> See `../templates/opportunity-entry.md` for the entry format. Many of these run on rolling or annual schedules — verify dates and pricing on the sponsor's site.

---

### RSNA Imaging AI Certificate Program

- **Sponsor:** Radiological Society of North America (RSNA)
- **Eligibility:** Open to RSNA members (free trainee membership available); appropriate for residents, fellows, and faculty
- **Deadline:** Rolling enrollment
- **Recurrence:** Continuous (cohort-based modules)
- **Link:** https://www.rsna.org/ai-certificate and https://www.rsna.org/ai-certificate/pricing-and-enrollment
- **Description:** Structured certificate program covering foundations of AI/ML in radiology, model evaluation, clinical deployment, and ethics. Designed for radiologists without formal data-science training.
- **Notes:** Useful entry point for trainees building an AI portfolio. Confirm current modules and certificate requirements before enrolling.
- **Verified:** 2026-04-27

### SIIM Educational Tracks (CIIP Prep, Hackathon, Webinars)

- **Sponsor:** Society for Imaging Informatics in Medicine (SIIM)
- **Eligibility:** Open; SIIM membership offers reduced rates and trainee discounts
- **Deadline:** Rolling
- **Recurrence:** Annual courses + ongoing webinars
- **Link:** https://siim.org
- **Description:** Educational programming spanning PACS administration, DICOM, AI deployment, and the Certified Imaging Informatics Professional (CIIP) credential prep. SIIM also runs an annual hackathon.
- **Notes:** CIIP itself is a non-physician certification, but the educational content is high-value for trainees pursuing informatics careers.
- **Verified:** 2026-04-27

### ACR Radiology Leadership Institute (RLI)

- **Sponsor:** American College of Radiology (ACR)
- **Eligibility:** Open to ACR members across career stages, including residents and fellows. Tiered programming for trainees vs. early-career vs. senior leaders.
- **Deadline:** Rolling for self-paced; specific cohort programs (e.g., Summit) have annual application cycles (verify annually)
- **Recurrence:** Continuous (online) + annual in-person Summit
- **Link:** https://www.acr.org/rli
- **Description:** Leadership development programming covering negotiation, finance, change management, and practice management. Trainee-targeted modules also available.
- **Notes:** Strong fit for trainees considering academic leadership, practice ownership, or administrative roles.
- **Verified:** 2026-04-27

### SIIM-ACR Data Science Summit

- **Sponsor:** Society for Imaging Informatics in Medicine (SIIM) and American College of Radiology (ACR) Data Science Institute
- **Eligibility:** Open to all; student pricing available ($100). General admission $250 (2026 rate).
- **Deadline:** 2026-06-09 (Summit date — register before to attend in-person or virtually)
- **Recurrence:** Annual (held alongside the SIIM Annual Meeting)
- **Link:** https://www.acr.org/Data-Science-and-Informatics/SIIM-DSI-Summit and https://annualmeeting.siim.org/register/#preconference-addons
- **Description:** One-day intensive on responsible AI adoption in radiology — governance, performance oversight, transparency, and real-world implementation. Sessions on agentic AI, automated draft reporting, ACR DSI initiatives, and economics/policy. Up to 5 AMA PRA Category 1 credits.
- **Notes:** Pairs naturally with SIIM Annual Meeting attendance (see conferences.md). Virtual option available for trainees who can't travel.
- **Verified:** 2026-04-27

### European Radiology Editorial & Review Fellowships (ESR)

- **Sponsor:** European Society of Radiology (ESR) — *European Radiology* journal
- **Eligibility:** Certified radiologists or residents; active ESR members with a research track record. Albert L. Baert Editorial Fellowship: under 40 years old. Yves Menu Review Fellowship: open to up to 15 candidates without age restriction.
- **Deadline:** (verify annually) — 2026 cycle closed November 7, 2025; next round opens early October 2026
- **Recurrence:** Annual
- **Link:** https://www.myesr.org/publications/fellowships/
- **Description:** Two complementary fellowships training early-career radiologists in editorial work (Baert: 2 positions, €2,250 stipend + free ECR registration) or peer review (Menu: up to 15 positions, free ECR registration). Open to North American applicants who hold ESR membership.
- **Notes:** Excellent CV-builder for trainees considering academic / journal-editorial careers. ESR membership available to residents and fellows globally — the trainee fee is modest. Companion to ECR (European Congress of Radiology) attendance.
- **Verified:** 2026-04-27
